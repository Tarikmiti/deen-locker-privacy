Privacy Policy

Deen Locker — Last updated: March 2026

In short: Deen Locker does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data We Collect

None. Deen Locker does not collect any personal information. All your data — prayer check-ins, reading progress, streaks, and settings — is stored locally on your device and (if enabled) synced to your Apple Watch via a direct device-to-device connection.

2. Location Data

Deen Locker may request access to your location to calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographic position. Your location data:

  • Is processed entirely on your device
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Can be replaced with manual city selection if you prefer not to share your location

3. Screen Time (Family Controls)

Deen Locker uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls / DeviceActivityMonitor) to optionally lock your device during prayer times. This feature:

  • Runs entirely through Apple's on-device framework
  • Does not give Deen Locker access to your app usage data
  • Does not transmit any information off your device
  • Can be disabled at any time in Settings

4. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. Deen Locker does not process or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles transactions.

5. Apple Watch

If you use the Apple Watch companion app, data is synced directly between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. No data passes through any external server.

6. Analytics & Tracking

Deen Locker does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. No Firebase, no Google Analytics, no ad networks. We do not track how you use the app.

7. Third-Party Services

Deen Locker does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data. The app operates fully offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

8. Data Storage

All data is stored locally using:

  • UserDefaults — for settings and preferences
  • App Groups — for sharing data between the app and its widgets (on-device only)

Your data is included in your iCloud/iTunes backups according to your device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Deen Locker does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since Deen Locker collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
